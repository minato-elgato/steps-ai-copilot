## What Is AIDE Search?

AIDE search is your intelligent gateway to finding information across all your connected data sources. Unlike traditional search engines, AIDE understands context, relationships, and natural language, making it easier to find exactly what you need from your emails, documents, conversations, and files.

**Why AIDE Search?** AIDE combines information from multiple sources, understands your intent, and provides contextual results that traditional search tools can’t match.

## How to Search in AIDE

Open Chat Interface

Navigate to the chat interface in your Steps AI workspace

Formulate Your Query

Ask your question in natural language, being as specific as possible about what you’re looking for

Select Data Sources

Choose which connected data sources to search through, or let AIDE search all sources automatically

Review Results

Review the intelligent responses and follow up with more specific questions if needed

## Search Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution |
| --- | --- |
| **No Results Found** | Try rephrasing your question or check if the data source is properly connected |
| **Irrelevant Results** | Be more specific in your query and provide additional context |
| **Slow Search** | Check your internet connection and ensure data sources are synced |
| **Missing Information** | Verify that the data source contains the information you’re looking for |

## FAQ

How does AIDE understand my search intent?

AIDE uses advanced natural language processing to understand the context and meaning behind your questions, not just keywords. It considers relationships between concepts and your conversation history.

Can I search across multiple data sources at once?

Yes! AIDE can search across all your connected data sources simultaneously, providing unified results that combine information from emails, documents, conversations, and more.

How accurate are the search results?

AIDE provides highly accurate results by understanding context and relationships. It ranks results by relevance and can explain why certain information was included.

Can I search for information from a specific time period?

Absolutely! You can specify time periods in your queries like “emails from last week” or “documents from Q3” to get time-relevant results.

What if I can't find what I'm looking for?

Try rephrasing your question, adding more context, or asking follow-up questions. AIDE can help you refine your search to find the right information.

Does AIDE search through file contents?

Yes! AIDE can search within document contents, not just file names. It can find information buried deep within spreadsheets, presentations, and other documents.

**Important**: Make sure your data sources are properly connected and synced for the best search results.
