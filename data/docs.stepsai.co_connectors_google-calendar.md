## Overview

The Google Calendar connector allows AIDE to access your calendar information, help schedule meetings, and provide intelligent scheduling assistance. This integration enables the AI to understand your availability and help coordinate with your schedule.

## Key Benefits

## Schedule Management

Access and manage your calendar events and appointments

## Meeting Coordination

Help schedule meetings and coordinate with participants

## Availability Check

Check your availability and suggest optimal meeting times

## Smart Scheduling

Get intelligent suggestions for meeting scheduling and coordination

## FAQ

Can AIDE schedule meetings automatically?

AIDE can help suggest meeting times and draft meeting invitations, but you’ll always review and confirm before anything is scheduled.

Can I connect multiple calendars?

Yes! You can connect multiple Google Calendars including work, personal, and shared team calendars.

How does AIDE handle time zones?

AIDE automatically considers time zones and can help coordinate meetings across different time zones.

Can AIDE see my private calendar events?

AIDE can only access calendar information when you specifically ask for scheduling help. It respects your privacy settings.
