# StepsAI

> StepsAI deploys AI agents that sell, support, and act across your website, WhatsApp, Instagram, Slack, and internal tools. One AI brain trained on your business, every channel, 5-minute install, no code required.

StepsAI gives businesses a single "AI brain" that can be embedded as a chat widget, popup, button, standalone page, or API, and connected to channels like WhatsApp, Instagram DM, and Microsoft Teams. The agent captures leads, answers product questions, tracks orders, books meetings, and triages support using the business's own knowledge base, product catalog, and integrations.
