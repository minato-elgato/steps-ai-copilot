### Step-by-Step Guide

1. **Find Slack Connector**:   - Look for Slack under “Communication”
   - Click the Slack card
   - Slack authentication page will open
2. **Connect Slack**:   - Sign in to your Slack workspace
   - Grant necessary permissions
   - Wait for status to change to “Connected”
   - Begin chatting with Slack data

## Security Note

- All connections are secure and encrypted
- You can revoke access at any time
- Permissions can be managed from your account settings
- Data access is read-only by default
