## Overview

Star Chats is a powerful organization feature that helps you mark and quickly access your most important conversations. Whether it’s a breakthrough solution, key project discussion, or valuable insight, starring conversations creates a curated collection of your most valuable interactions with AIDE.

## Key Benefits

## Quick Access

Instantly access your most important conversations without searching

## Curated Collection

Build a personal library of your most valuable insights and solutions

## Project Organization

Group related conversations by starring them for easy project reference

## Knowledge Management

Create a searchable archive of your best AI interactions and solutions

## Getting Started

## FAQ

How many conversations can I star?

You can star unlimited conversations. However, we recommend being selective to maintain a high-quality reference library that’s easy to navigate.

Can I organize starred chats into categories?

Currently, starred chats are displayed in chronological order. You can use the search function to find specific types of starred conversations quickly.

What happens if I unstar a conversation?

Unstarring removes the conversation from your starred collection, but it remains in your regular chat history. You can always star it again later.

Can I share starred conversations with my team?

Yes! You can export starred conversations and share them with team members, or share the conversation links directly if your platform supports it.

How do I find a specific starred conversation?

Use the search function within the starred section, or browse chronologically. You can also search by keywords that you remember from the conversation.

Should I star every useful conversation?

Be selective! Star only the most valuable conversations to maintain a clean, useful reference library. Too many starred chats can make it harder to find what you need.

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution | Prevention |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Can’t Find Starred Section** | Check sidebar navigation and ensure you’re in the correct view | Familiarize yourself with the interface layout |
| **Star Not Working** | Try refreshing the page and clicking the star icon again | Ensure stable internet connection |
| **Too Many Starred Chats** | Review and unstar less important conversations | Be selective about what you star from the beginning |
| **Search Not Finding Starred** | Try different keywords or check if the conversation is actually starred | Use memorable terms and verify starring status |
