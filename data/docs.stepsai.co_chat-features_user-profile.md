## Step-by-Step Guide

1. **Using the Side Navigation**:   - Look for the theme toggle icon in the left sidebar
   - Click to switch between light and dark themes
   - The change applies immediately to the entire interface
2. **Using Settings Page**:   - Navigate to Settings
   - Find the Display section
   - Select your preferred theme from the dropdown
   - Choose between Light, Dark, or System (follows your device settings)

## Benefits

- Reduce eye strain in different lighting conditions
- Customize your workspace to your preferences
- Seamlessly sync with your system theme if desired
