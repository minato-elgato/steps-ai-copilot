## Overview

The Outlook Agent provides comprehensive Microsoft 365 integration, managing both email and calendar functions within your enterprise environment. Perfect for organizations using the Microsoft ecosystem.

## Email Management

Professional email composition with enterprise templates, advanced search and filtering across your mailbox, contact management and relationship tracking, meeting scheduling with Teams integration, and document access through SharePoint integration

## Calendar Coordination

Multi-calendar management across teams and departments, meeting room booking and resource allocation, travel time calculation and scheduling optimization, recurring meeting management with pattern recognition, and availability coordination across time zones

## Enterprise Integration

Microsoft Teams meeting creation and management, SharePoint document access and collaboration, OneDrive file integration and sharing, Active Directory user management and permissions, and Exchange server integration and compliance

## FAQ

Can it work with my organization's security policies?

Yes! The Outlook Agent is designed for enterprise environments and respects your organization’s security policies, compliance requirements, and access controls.

Does it integrate with Microsoft Teams?

Absolutely! The agent can create Teams meetings, manage meeting settings, and coordinate with your Teams workspace seamlessly.

Can it access SharePoint documents?

Yes, with proper permissions. The agent can help you find, access, and share documents stored in your organization’s SharePoint sites.

How does it handle multiple calendars?

The agent can manage multiple calendars, including shared calendars, resource calendars, and team calendars, providing a unified view of all your scheduling needs.

Can it help with compliance and retention policies?

Yes! The agent understands enterprise compliance requirements and can help manage email retention, archiving, and regulatory compliance.

Does it work with Exchange Online and on-premises?

The agent works with both Exchange Online and on-premises Exchange servers, adapting to your organization’s email infrastructure.

Can it manage distribution lists and groups?

Yes! The agent can help you manage distribution lists, security groups, and Microsoft 365 groups for efficient communication.

How does it handle sensitive information?

The agent respects your organization’s data classification and sensitivity labels, ensuring proper handling of confidential information.
